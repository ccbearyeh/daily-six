i18n showcase

  • This is the same Settings view as zh-TW, rendered with locale = "en". Compare the language pill in the top right: EN active here, 中 in other screens.
  • All visible strings come from src/locales/en.json. No hard-coded literals in JSX (enforced by ESLint).
  • Manifest-level strings (extension name, action tooltip) come from _locales/en/messages.json via chrome.i18n — see SPEC.md §9.1 for why we need both systems.
  • Switching language toggle here updates settings.locale in chrome.storage.local and calls i18n.changeLanguage() — no page reload.
  • Date format ("Mon, May 25") follows Intl.DateTimeFormat(locale); in zh-TW it becomes "2026-05-25 (一)".
